INDUSTRI SANDRA

We are a company of Colombian origin, founded in 1999 by a group of entrepreneurs who, thanks to their entrepreneurship and experience in the sector of Services of Cleaning and Institutional Maintenance, decided to create a manufacturing company of Industrial Products with high standards of quality and efficiency . Over the years Biggest Industries has expanded its product portfolio to meet the demands of its customers while maintaining the same philosophy of quality in the product and service. We are manufacturers of all the range of chemical products for the toilet, covered under the brand BIGGEST, with high standards of quality and wide support. We offer an Integral Solution to your needs of supplies for the toilet and cafeteria. We offer personalized attention so that you select the best option when buying your supplies. We handle permanent promotional plans which represent additional savings benefits for your organization.

Our Advantages

Mission

Our history

  • 1991-1995 As a consequence of the economic opening in Colombia we began our distribution of AVIGAL HENNA product, introducing and marketing in modern channels between 1991 and 1995.
  • 1996 - 2000 Due to our experience in distribution, the brands FREEMAN and BANANA BOAT choose us as their exclusive distributor in Colombia and we also started imports of the most complete line of Cosmetic Accessories for the care of the face, hands, feet and body, brand FREESTYLE.
  • 2002 - 2004 Between 2002 and 2003 the brands Aromasense, Line of fragrances, liquid soaps and antibacterial Gels were launched. And from 2004 onwards products such as Depilex depilatory creams, Nutrit Shampoo and Sanity Plus Liquid Soaps have been developed and launched.
  • 2008 Belleza Express S.A. Becomes part of the Top 10 of the leading companies in the Health and Beauty business.
  • TODAY Belleza Express SA is one of the fastest growing companies with health care products in Colombia. Today we have 630 workers.
